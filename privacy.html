Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Overview

LifeOS (“we”, “our”) is a personal assistant app that helps you manage tasks, health (e.g. hydration, sleep), and calendar. This policy describes how we handle your information.

2. Data we use

  • Account & profile: When you sign in with Google, we use your email and calendar/Gmail access only to provide the app’s features (e.g. syncing events, triaging email).
  • Local data: Tasks, logs, and preferences are stored on your device. If you use a backend you control, some data may be synced there.
  • AI: If you use AI features, your inputs and context may be sent to our backend or third‑party AI services to generate responses.

3. Sharing

We do not sell your data. Data may be shared only as needed to run the service (e.g. with Google for sign‑in and calendar, or with AI providers when you use AI features).

4. Your choices

You can disconnect Google in the app, clear local data, or stop using the app at any time.

5. Contact

For privacy questions, use the support contact you have for LifeOS (e.g. from the app or website).
