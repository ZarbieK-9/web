LifeOS

Your personal AI assistant for tasks, health, and daily organization.

What LifeOS does

LifeOS is a mobile app that helps you:

  • Manage tasks — Create, complete, and organize to-dos with filters and priorities.
  • Track health — Log hydration, monitor sleep, and use focus timers.
  • Connect your calendar — View and manage Google Calendar events.
  • Triage email — See Gmail inbox and categories (when you connect Gmail).
  • Chat with AI — Get help from PicoClaw (e.g. plan your day, add tasks, answer questions).
  • Partner sync — Share snippets and status with a partner via the app.

Why we ask for your data

To provide these features, LifeOS may ask for:

  • Google sign-in — So we can show your Calendar and Gmail (read/write for calendar, read/modify for Gmail). We use this only to sync and display your events and emails inside the app.
  • Tasks and logs — Stored on your device and, if you use our backend, synced there so you can access them across sessions.
  • Messages you send to the AI — Processed to answer questions and run actions (e.g. creating tasks).

We do not sell your data. For full details, see our Privacy Policy below.
